# 💰 MercurX Token

MERX is a multi-functional token as it provides utility (e.g., guaranteed allocation), and enables membership (e.g., IDOs) in the MercurX universe. For the next phases, other MERX-related tokens are planned and may be introduced into the ecosystem.

* Symbol: MERX
* Decimals: 9
* Total Supply (Hard Cap): 1,000,000,000 (1B)
* Blockchain: Binance Smart Chain (BSC)
