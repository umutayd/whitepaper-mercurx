# 📔 Problem Statement

Cryptocurrency investments are currently marred by three significant challenges:

1. An alarming lack of transparency
2. Questionable sustainability of numerous projects
3. An unsatisfactory return on investments.

 

Historically, most crypto-assets have been privy to a select group of large investors, often leaving small investors in the dark. This inequitable system not only impedes small investors from seizing lucrative opportunities but also threatens the integrity and sustainability of the crypto ecosystem.

 

Most of the crypto-assets issued to date have generally been presented to large investors behind closed doors, with the very high portion of the investment return delivered in the timeframe between these behind-the-scenes talks up to the General Public Sales phases. On the other hand, small investors were generally unaware of these “pre-investments” at previous stages. Even the minority who were aware of the opportunities were not able to participate to these investment rounds due to having difficulty in meeting the high prerequisites required to take part.

 

With the development of platforms with transparent and public decision mechanisms, small investors started to participate in the market and receive returns from their investments in a fair way. However, the sustainability of these projects presented themselves as the next big issue. There is, therefore, a demand in the market for platforms that satisfy both the transparency need as well as sustainability of the projects presented.

 

### Solution – MercurX

Addressing these challenges head-on, MercurX pledges to offer a transparent, reliable, and sustainable solution. Harnessing the unparalleled reliability of blockchain technology, MercurX presents a sanctuary for investors where investment records are meticulously maintained. \*Moreover, it expands its horizons beyond popular sectors, fostering initiatives in sectors like Energy, Tourism, Manufacturing, and Retail.

 

Investors can transparently negotiate more favorable terms and maximize the return on their investment on this platform.

 

MercurX customers can easily create crypto assets related to their projects using this platform and present them to investors. All these steps are executed completely, safely and transparently, and the historical records of these transactions are also securely stored thanks to blockchain technology.

 

Unlike other platforms, MercurX also encompasses different and important sectors besides De-Fi, Gaming, and Metaverse by supporting, offering and prioritizing these projects. Some sample industries in this regard are Energy, Tourism, Manufacturing, and Retail. We will not only offer projects in these industries, but MercurX will also create awareness about these projects and support the use cases of the projects in both enterprise settings and in other crypto platforms.
